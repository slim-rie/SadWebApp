About JBR Tanching C.O

Your Trusted Partner in Sewing Since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2014, JBR Tanching C.O has been at the forefront of providing high-quality sewing machines, parts, and fabrics to the Filipino community. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in the sewing industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the Philippines.

Our commitment to quality, customer service, and competitive pricing has made us the go-to destination for both professional tailors and hobbyists alike.

Our Mission

We strive to empower creativity and craftsmanship by providing reliable sewing equipment and exceptional service. Our mission is to support the growing community of sewers, tailors, and fashion enthusiasts in the Philippines by offering:

  • High-quality sewing machines and parts
  • Premium fabrics and materials
  • Expert technical support
  • Competitive pricing
  • Comprehensive after-sales service
